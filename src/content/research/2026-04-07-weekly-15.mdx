In this week, the main job is to rebuild and upgrade the optical system of bio-sensing table. 

## What I did

* Tested the SLM optical path in a 450x150mm breadboard. This breadboard includes a HWP, a beam expander, a SLM, two lenses, a D-shape mirror( to separate 0-th order diffraction beam and 1-th order beam after the SLM) and several mirrors. Now this modular board is ready to transplant to the bio-sensing table at anytime.
* Bought some optomechanical components from lbtek. This is to change the way of building and fixing optical component -- instead of relying on the friction of sleeve to lock the optical post, now we directly use a fixed-length optical post and spacers to determine the component height.
* Rebuilt the optical path in the bio-sensing table. Now use breadboard to build modular optical system. 
* Tried to build double-pass AOM system.
